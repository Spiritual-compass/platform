Today's Sacred Insight

"The present moment is the only time over which we have dominion."
— Thích Nhất Hạnh
Buddhism Presence

Daily Reflection

In our busy world, we often live anywhere but the present moment — lost in memories of the past or anxieties about the future. This Buddhist teaching reminds us that true power, peace, and spiritual growth can only happen right here, right now. Today, practice returning to the present moment whenever you notice your mind wandering. What does it feel like to be fully here?
