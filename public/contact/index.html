Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of spiritual guidance do you provide?

We offer respectful, non-judgmental guidance that honors all sincere spiritual seeking. Our approach is educational and supportive rather than directive - we help you explore options and find what resonates with your heart and values.

Do you favor one religious tradition over others?

No. We respect and learn from all sincere spiritual traditions - Christianity, Islam, Judaism, Buddhism, Hinduism, Indigenous wisdom, and secular philosophy. Our goal is to help you find the path that authentically fits your spiritual seeking.

Is this service free?

Yes, all our guidance and resources are provided freely. We believe spiritual support should be accessible to everyone, regardless of financial circumstances.

Are you qualified to provide spiritual guidance?

Our team includes individuals with diverse spiritual backgrounds, formal religious education, and years of experience in interfaith dialogue. However, we always recommend also connecting with local spiritual communities and qualified religious leaders in your tradition of interest.

What if I'm in a spiritual crisis?

We take spiritual crises seriously and prioritize these messages. However, if you're experiencing thoughts of self-harm, please contact a mental health crisis line immediately. We can provide spiritual support alongside professional mental health care.

Can you help me choose a religion?

We can help you explore different traditions, understand their core teachings, and reflect on what might resonate with your spiritual seeking. However, the choice of spiritual path is deeply personal - we provide information and support, but the decision is always yours.
