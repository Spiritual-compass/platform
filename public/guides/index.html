Your First Week

A simple day-by-day framework for anyone starting completely from scratch.

  1. Day 1 — Sit still for five minutes. No technique required. Just stop, close your eyes, and notice what's happening inside you.
  2. Day 2 — Read one short sacred text. A psalm, a Zen koan, a verse of the Tao Te Ching. Let it sit with you through the day.
  3. Day 3 — Take the assessment. Use the Spiritual Compass assessment to understand where your instincts lean.
  4. Day 4 — Try one guided meditation. Visit the meditation page and use the Start Practice button on any exercise.
  5. Day 5 — Explore one tradition in depth. Choose the tradition from the assessment results and spend thirty minutes on its page.
  6. Day 6 — Create a small ritual. Light a candle, take three breaths, set an intention. Do it at the same time each day this week.
  7. Day 7 — Reflect. What resonated? What felt foreign? What surprised you? Write three sentences in a journal.
