We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Our Commitment to Privacy

At Spiritual Compass, we believe your privacy is sacred. We are committed to protecting your personal information and ensuring you have a positive experience on our site. This policy explains our privacy practices in simple, transparent language.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it to us:

  • Contact Forms: When you contact us, we collect your name, email, and message content
  • Newsletter Signup: When you subscribe to daily wisdom, we collect your email address
  • Spiritual Assessment: When you take our assessment, we collect your responses to help provide personalized recommendations
  • Website Analytics: We use basic analytics to understand how visitors use our site (no personally identifying information)
  • Cookies: We use minimal cookies only to improve your browsing experience

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your messages and provide spiritual guidance
  • Send you the daily wisdom newsletter (if you subscribed)
  • Personalize your spiritual assessment results
  • Improve our website and services
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never: Sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your spiritual journey is confidential.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information:

  • All data is encrypted in transit using SSL/TLS security
  • We use secure servers to store your information
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review our security practices

However, no online platform is completely secure. While we do our best to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Newsletter & Communications

When you subscribe to our daily wisdom newsletter:

  • You'll receive thoughtful spiritual quotes and teachings
  • You can unsubscribe at any time with a single click
  • We will never share your email with other organizations
  • We'll occasionally send updates about new content or features (rarely)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any personal information we have about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (with exceptions for legal obligations)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from communications at any time
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@spiritual-compass.com

7. Children's Privacy

Spiritual Compass does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at privacy@spiritual-compass.com

8. Third-Party Services

Our website uses certain third-party services:

  • Netlify: Hosts our website and processes form submissions
  • Google Fonts: Provides typography
  • Analytics Services: Help us understand website usage

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies minimally, only to:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Track basic analytics (anonymized)
  • Improve your browsing experience

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

10. Spiritual Communication Confidentiality

We treat all communications sent through our contact forms as confidential spiritual conversations. Your messages about your spiritual journey are protected with the same respect you would receive from a trusted spiritual advisor. We will never use your personal spiritual information for marketing or share it publicly.

11. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date on this page. Your continued use of the site indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
