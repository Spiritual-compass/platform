# Feta Pesto Chicken with Rice

## Ingredients
- 2 chicken breasts
- 100g feta cheese, crumbled
- 3 tbsp green pesto (basil)
- 1 cup rice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder
- Cherry tomatoes (optional)
- Fresh basil for garnish

## Method
1. Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F)
2. Butterfly or pound chicken breasts to even thickness
3. Season with salt, pepper, garlic powder
4. Spread pesto over the chicken, top with crumbled feta
5. Bake 20-25 min until chicken is cooked through (75°C internal)
6. Cook rice — use chicken stock instead of water for extra flavour
7. Serve chicken sliced over rice, spoon any pan juices on top

## Tips
- Stuffing the pesto & feta *inside* a butterflied breast keeps it extra juicy
- Roast cherry tomatoes alongside for 15 min — they burst and make a natural sauce
- A squeeze of lemon at the end ties it all together
